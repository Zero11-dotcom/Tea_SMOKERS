About us

Not a regular cafe. A chai ritual built for repeat love.

We are the Teasmokers, offering one of the finest blends of tea and delicious snacks. We are not like any regular cafe in town where people simply chill, although you are welcome to do that too. Our versions of tea are made to give you second thoughts about your regular chai.
